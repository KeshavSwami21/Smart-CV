**Job Overview:**  
Samsung is looking for an experienced AI/ML Engineer to join our dynamic team. The role involves designing, developing, and optimizing AI-driven solutions for Samsung’s next-generation products and services.

**Key Responsibilities:**  
- Develop and implement AI/ML models for various applications, including computer vision, natural language processing (NLP), and recommendation systems.  
- Optimize deep learning models for deployment on edge devices, mobile platforms, and cloud infrastructure.  
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI technologies into Samsung’s products and services.  
- Conduct research to stay updated with the latest advancements in AI and ML, and apply innovative techniques to enhance existing models.  
- Utilize large-scale datasets for training, testing, and fine-tuning machine learning models.  
- Design and implement scalable AI architectures and pipelines.  
- Perform model evaluation, debugging, and performance tuning to improve accuracy and efficiency.  
- Document AI solutions, frameworks, and best practices for internal and external stakeholders.  

**Required Qualifications:**  
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or a related field. A Ph.D. is a plus.  
- Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C++, or Java.  
- Hands-on experience with machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, or Keras.  
- Knowledge of deep learning architectures, including CNNs, RNNs, Transformers, and GANs.  
- Experience with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) and distributed computing frameworks.  
- Understanding of data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model deployment techniques.  
- Proficiency in using tools like Docker, Kubernetes, and ONNX for AI model deployment.  
- Excellent problem-solving skills, analytical thinking, and ability to work in a fast-paced environment.  

**Preferred Qualifications:**
